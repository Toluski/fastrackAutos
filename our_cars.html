Ready for urban adventure?
Range Rover Evoque is the perfect vehicle to explore the city, putting performance, power and outstanding design at your fingertips. You’ve never driven a car like it. Begin your adventure by choosing from three unique models: the Pure, Prestige and Dynamic.

Capable in all situations
 More than just a pretty face, Range Rover Evoque can take anything the city can throw at it. It’s the Land Rover created for urban living.

The Mercedes-Benz V8 Biturbo engines are characterized by compelling dynamics and an exceptional degree of efficiency.
On the one hand, the engines provide a stirring driving experience thanks to rated outputs of up to 577 hp and maximum torque of 664 lb/ft . On the other hand, they allow for a reduction in fuel consumption thanks to direct fuel injection with spray-guided combustion, twin turbochargers, and alternator management.
