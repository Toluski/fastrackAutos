WHAT TYPES OF VEHICLES DO YOU OFFER? [+]

Our standard fleet features Lamborghini and Rover products. However, we also have many other fine car and truck types, depending on the location where you. For this reason, our online reservation process is the easiest way to see available cars at your particular location.

CAN I RESERVE A SPECIFIC MAKE/MODEL CAR? [+]
Yes. Although our standard fleet is not reservable by specific make and model, our Street Fleet is. Book your choice of any of our Street Fleet vehicles, and we will do our best to have that exact car waiting for you at the pick-up counter.

DO YOU HAVE 4-WHEEL DRIVE VEHICLES? [+]

Our extensive fleet includes mid-size to large SUVs, many of which offer 4WD. We also offer a limited number of Hummer H3 SUVs for the ultimate 4WD experience.

DO YOU OFFER HYBRID AND LOW-EMISSION VEHICLES? [+]
We offer hybrids at many of our locations. Our SmartWaySM Certified Green Machines are eco-friendly and get great fuel mileage as well.
